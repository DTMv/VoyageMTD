Toscana - Italy

I love Toscana ! Indeed, Tuscany is a region of Italy steeped in history. I strongly advise you to visit Firenze. You could also go through Milan, the fashion capital combining modernity with authenticity. Venice can also prove to be a good getaway; just like Rome.

Albania

Magnificent landscapes for beautiful walks inland. It is also possible to have a good holiday by the sea. Life is inexpensive and the Albanians are very welcoming and friendly.

Canada

My best trip! I went to the Toronto area. We discover a universe close to American culture. But the people I was able to meet are welcoming and made me discover the wonders of this city. From the cruise to the CNN Tower to Niagara Falls. I strongly recommend this region.

Fuerteventura - Canary's Island

Surfing enthusiasts? This island is for you! Paradise island with monstrous waves perfect for surfing.
